MagicVideo V2

Multi-Stage High-Aesthetic Video Generation

arXiv

Abstract

The growing demand for high-fidelity video generation from textual descriptions has catalyzed significant research in this field. In this paper, we introduce MagicVideo V2, an innovative multi-stage Text-to-Image-to-Video (T2I2V) system that seamlessly integrates state-of-the-art Text-to-Image, spatial base module, motion module, reference image feature extractors, and frame interpolation all together to form a comprehensive end-to-end system. This system not only generates a high aesthetic start image based on a given text prompt, but also transforms this image and the accompanying text into an aesthetically pleasing, high-resolution video with remarkable fidelity and smoothness. MagicVideo V2 demonstrates superior performance over leading Text-to-Video systems such as Runway, Pika 1.0, Morph, Moon Valley, and SVD with clear-margined GSB (Good/Same/Bad) scores that are calculated by 61 independent human voters based on unbiasly selected 100 text prompts as the evaluation case.
